Our Vision

ASTRA Labs exists to provide Seneca students with hands-on experience in rocket design, fabrication, and testing. We believe that the best way to learn aerospace engineering is by building real systems that fly.

  • Communication: To reach our goals, we must communicate with our team members on a regular basis.

  • Respect: Team members must demonstrate respect for each other. We have zero tolerance for bullying and harassment.

  • Collaboration: Our projects are huge and cannot be completed by one person alone.


Why Rocketry?

Rocketry is one of the most challenging and interdisciplinary fields in engineering. A successful rocket launch requires expertise in:

  • Aerodynamics and fluid dynamics
  • Structural analysis and materials science
  • Propulsion and combustion engineering
  • Electronics and avionics
  • Control systems and telemetry
  • Safety protocols and risk management
  • Project management and teamwork
  • Manufacturing and fabrication

By working on rockets, our members gain real-world skills that directly translate to careers in aerospace, defense, and advanced manufacturing.
