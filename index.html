Who We Are

We are a student-led team at Seneca Polytechnic. Our team consists of members who are passionate about the space innovation industry. We began our journey in 2025, but despite the fact that we are a very recent group, we have massive ambitions.

Why Join Us?

Here are just a few of the major benefits of joining Astra Labs:

- Enhance Your Resume

- Work on real tech like rockets and payloads

- Participate in Launch Canada

- Educate Yourself

- Network with Industry Professionals

Join Us!

We encourage all Seneca students to be a part of our group, whether that means assisting in the construction of our projects or just keeping up to date with our progress! Anyone attending Seneca Polytechnic is free to join, regardless of their program. All we ask is that you have a passion for innovation and technology, especially in the space industry.
