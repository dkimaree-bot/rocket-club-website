Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need experience to join?

Not at all! ASTRA Labs welcomes students from all programs and skill levels. We provide training and mentorship to help you learn.

Does my Program have to be STEM Related?

No. We encourage all Seneca students to join. While having a STEM background can help, all we ask is that you are interested in science and innovation in the space sector.

Do I need to be a Seneca Student to Join?

Yes, unfortunately, due to SSF policy, only students attending Seneca Polytechnic can join.

How Long am I required to Participate?

To receive a reference, we require that students dedicate at least 6 months of participation in our club

Can I join mid-semester?

Yes! We accept new members year-round. Reach out via email or social media to get started.
